How to Play

In Lethal, your goal is simple: defeat the enemy in just 1 turn.

  • You'll be dealt a few cards with different effects.
  • Drag and drop them in the right order to bring the enemy's HP to zero.
  • Each card costs energy — spend it wisely.
  • Some cards apply debuffs like Vulnerable or effects that play the next card twice.
  • Share with your friends how quickly you defeated the enemy!

New puzzle every week. Come back and have fun!
